TV Girl is an American indie pop band from San Diego, California. TV Girl frequently samples songs
and media from the 1960s in their music. The band employs a genre not easily defined, with major
inspiration from plunderphonics, indie pop, lo-fi and electronic music found in most of their works,
while taking on more trip-hop-like elements in other works. Altogether, the band proclaims themself
as a "hypnotic pop" group, due to their use of sampling, keyboards, and reverb effects. The band
was upset when their music was labeled "sun-drenched California pop," pointing out that there are no
lyrical allusions in their music that warrant the title. Lyrically, a majority of TV Girl's discography
revolves around love and ships.Their subject matter is nostalgic and sad, but simultaneously sarcastic
and humorous. Some motifs in the band's lyrics include heartbreak, cynicism, memories, cigarettes,
hair, sex, women's first names, and loneliness.
